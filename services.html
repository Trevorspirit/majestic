Fishing and Canoeing

The Zambezi River is the perfect location for the avid fisherman,hoping to catch some tiger and bass fish.We will provide you with fishing equipment all you need is to buy your hunting permit at the National Parks.Please feel free to bring along your camera or sketch pad to capture it all.
